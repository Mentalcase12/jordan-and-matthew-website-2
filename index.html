Love Letter — Read slowly 💌

I love you so much, my baby. My Jordy.

It's crazy to think it's been five months already. Sometimes it feels unreal, you know? But in the best way. Like, how has it only been five months? It feels like I've known you forever. And even with all the distance, just being online, this feels more real than anything I've ever had.

I seriously look forward to our calls all day. Just waiting to see your face pop up on my screen. All the stupid stuff from my day just... disappears. It's just you. Nothing else matters. Every time we laugh late at night, every sleepy 'i love you' before we pass out on the call... those are my favorite moments. It's insane how you can be so far away but still make me feel this close and this safe.

I love every single thing we do. Even just talking for hours about nothing. And I love our dumb Roblox nights, whether we're trying to be all chill in Grow a Garden or just dying laughing in Violence District. And don't even get me started on The Forest and Sons of the Forest. Us trying to survive and build a base while freaking out... it's the best. Honestly, I don't even care what we're playing. It's just getting to be with you, hanging out in our own little world. You just make everything fun.

I dunno, I just wanted to write all this out. I really wanted to try and make you feel appreciated, even if it's just a little bit. It's not some fancy gift, but it's just... me. It's from my heart. I hope when you read this you can feel even a little bit of how much I care about you.

You mean everything to me, Jordy. You're my favorite person, my reason to smile, my peace. You've shown me what real love actually feels like. It's not just the butterfly thing (though I still get that when you smile). It's the comfort. It's knowing I can be 100% myself. It's how you make everything feel okay, even when life is hard and this distance sucks. Just hearing your voice can calm me down.

I feel like I love you more every single day. It's crazy. Sometimes I honestly feel like my heart is just gonna explode with how much I adore you. I never knew I could love anyone like this. You're such a huge part of me now, and I wouldn't trade this for anything.

I am counting the literal seconds until the day I can hold you for real, not just through a phone. I promise you, one day I'm going to hug you so tight and finally feel you in my arms. I'm going to kiss your forehead and hold your hand, and I am never letting go.

Until then, I'm right here. I'll keep loving you with everything I have. 💞
