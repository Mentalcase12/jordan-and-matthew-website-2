My dearest Jordan,

       

I love you so much, my baby. Sometimes I still can’t believe we’ve been together for this long, it feels unreal in the best way possible. Even though everything between us has been online, it feels more real than anything else I’ve ever known. Every video call, every late night laugh, every sleepy “I love you” before bed… they’ve all become the moments I look forward to the most every single day. When I see your face pop up on my screen, everything else just kinda fades away. It’s crazy how someone miles away can make me feel so close, so safe, and so loved.

       

I made this small thing because I wanted to make you feel appreciated, even if it’s just a little bit. It’s not fancy or expensive, but it’s from my heart and I hope that when you read this, you feel even a fraction of how much I care about you. You mean so much to me, my baby. You are my world, my peace, my reason to smile when I wake up. You’ve shown me what real love feels like, not just the butterflies, but the comfort, the laughter, the way you make everything feel okay even when life gets hard.

       

Every day that passes, my love for you grows even more. Sometimes I think my heart’s gonna explode because of how much I adore you. I never knew I could love someone this deeply until I met you. You’ve become such a huge part of me, and I wouldn’t trade what we have for anything. One day, I’ll get to hold you for real, not just through a screen, and when that day comes, I’m never letting go. Until then, I’ll keep loving you with all my heart. 💞
